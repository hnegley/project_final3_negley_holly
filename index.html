The only place to get Rataouille

We are proudly known as one of the finest restaurants in all of Paris. Our specialty is Rataouille, perfected by our top chef Remy. Our former owner and founder, Gusteau, is the writer of the famous cookbook "Anyone Can Cook." His son, Alfredo Linguini, has masterfully taken over the restaurant and has maintained it's 5 star rating.
